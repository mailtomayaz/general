iota Outdoors solves problems. Problems that some might consider small but make significant differences when resolved. We refuse to be an industry me-too, and we will only release a product that proves to provide cutting-edge solutions for hunters or shooters. Our products are invented for the inventors -- outdoorsmen who identify ways to enhance their shooting experience with modern technology and creative thinking.

 

DERRICK RATLIFF

PRESIDENT

The president of iota Outdoors, Derrick Ratliff, grew up hunting, fishing and working on a family farm in Boyd, Texas. As part of the farming lifestyle, he learned to build, repair and customize mechanical implements. A BS graduate in wildlife sciences from Texas A&M University with a minor in business, he was a competitive college archer who was ranked No. 13 in the nation. Following graduation, Derrick worked as the R&D Manager for Bowtech Archery and Octane and as the Wholesale Marketing Manager for Ranch Hand Truck Accessories. He was also the producer and host of the Sportsman Channel TV series "Ranch Hand Horizons” prior to founding the custom rifle manufacturing company Horizon Firearms. Derrick is an avid hunter and shooter who brings extensive field experience to iota Outdoors' vision and business execution.

 

AUSTIN RATLIFF

PROJECT COORDINATOR

Austin Ratliff is the project coordinator for iota Outdoors and is the company’s jack of all trades. Austin grew up the youngest of three Ratliff brothers on a farm and ranch in Boyd, Texas. Hunting, fishing, working and fixing things were a by-product of the stereotypical Texas upbringing. Austin received a visualization degree from Texas A&M University’s College of Architecture with a focus in 3-D animation and computer graphics. His dedication to detail and visual aesthetics as well as his unique set of computer skills provide great value to product development and marketing at iota Outdoors. Austin’s dream hunt includes an Arctic Wolf with his totally tricked out Horizon Firearms 6.5 Creedmoor.

 

MCKENZIE CROW

OFFICE MANAGER

McKenzie Crow is the office manager and often serves as the iota Outdoors “mama.” Keeping the boys in check, McKenzie provides great value in customer service and accounting. After earning a kinesiology major with minors in psychology and sports management from Texas A&M University, McKenzie joined iota Outdoors full-time because she saw the enormous potential for career and business growth. McKenzie grew up being the only girl in a neighborhood full of boys, which shaped her love for hunting and the outdoors. A five-animal hunt in Africa featuring a leopard most often visits her hunting dreams.

iota Outdoors is headquartered in College Station, Texas, along with its sister company, Horizon Firearms, and is a subsidiary of Kaspar Companies.

About Kaspar Companies

With over a century of experience, we're proud to say that our family of businesses spans 20 recessions, one depression, two world wars, oil embargoes, steel shortages and 20 presidential administrations. Starting with a single wire basket, Kaspar Companies has since grown to be the parent company of eight subsidiaries: Ranch Hand Truck Accessories, Kaspar Manufacturing, Texas Precious Metals, BEDROCK Truck Beds, Truckfitters, Silverback Homes, iota Outdoors and Horizon Firearms. A fifth-generation family business headquartered in Shiner, Texas, we have evolved and expanded into diverse industries while remaining grounded in the founding principles of quality American workmanship and honest business practices.
